CLOVES&CRUNCHIES

Online spice souk...

Driven by our passion for cooking and eating healthy, flavorful meals, Cloves&Crunchies was born out of the love for spices and our experiences across Africa and and the middle east spice market. We have whole and ground spices, spices blend and tea mix and supply a wide range of gourmet spices across the country. At Cloves&Crunchies, we promote healthy eating through making accessible our premium quality aromatic spices with just a tap of your finger. Our mission is to promote using spices, not only as seasoning but for their medicinal benefits as well.
